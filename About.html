About Me

I am a New Jersey native who grew up in Elizabeth, NJ, until I finished college and took my first job that brought me to Cincinnati, Ohio (pictured left) and eventually Syracuse, NY (pictured right). I graduated from the New Jersey Institute of Technology with a B.S. in Computer Engineering and a technical specificiation in Telecommunications. I decided to major in Computer Engineering due to my most profound childhood memory. As a kid, I was always fascinated by the trains used by NJ Transit and the MTA. The start of my interest in engineering comes from waiting for (what is now known as) the D train with my father, and asking him who drives the trains we always see. The answer is of course, An engineer. The title just stuck with me growing up and once I saw I could combine my love for computers into a different discipline of engineering, I decided upon my path. To this day, I always make it a mission to visit a city or town's transit system to snap a photo of the trains they use.
